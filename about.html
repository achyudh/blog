My Projects

I am currently working on an analysis framework to decode online communities. The primary focus of this project is on decoding the rich history and the complex community structure present in popular online communities, by choosing mailing lists and IRC channels as a case study.

I have also worked on developing Monte-Carlo methods to assess the feasibility of biofuel production. This involved the simulation of various aspects of a biorefinery and its environment to forecast its profitability.

My first exposure to Artificial Neural Networks (ANN) and Support Vector Machines (SVM) was when I was analysing various forecasting models to determine the breakeven for a bank. This study compared the machine learning based forecasting methods to the more traditional approach, the Autoregressive Integrated Moving Average (ARIMA) model.
